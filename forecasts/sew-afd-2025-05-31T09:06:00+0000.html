-- Changed Discussion --
Showers return to western WA
today as an upper level trough cuts through the Pac NW. Most of
the shower activity will be along the coast this morning with
showers reaching the interior and Cascades by 20-21Z per HREF

.AVIATION...
-- Changed Discussion --
Southwest flow aloft continues today as an upper trough
and weakening frontal system approach the area. Ceilings will lower
to MVFR at times in light showers in the vicinity of the front along
the coast and across interior areas mainly KPAE northward from 15Z

expected this afternoon and evening near the coast and areas
adjacent to the Strait of Juan de Fuca.

.MARINE...
-- Changed Discussion --
Onshore flow will increase later today into Sunday as
surface ridging builds strongly offshore behind an exiting frontal
system. This will lead to gusty northwest winds and choppy seas
across the coastal waters as well as a westerly push through the
strait. This general pattern remains in place into early next week
with onshore flow gradually easing as the offshore ridge weakens
toward the middle of next week.

The persistent onshore flow will generate seas at or above 10 feet
over portions of the outer coastal waters tonight into Sunday along
with some fairly short periods for steep seas. 27
-- End Changed Discussion --
