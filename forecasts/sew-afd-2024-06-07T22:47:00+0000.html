.SHORT TERM /TONIGHT THROUGH MONDAY/...
-- Changed Discussion --
Sunny skies and warmer
temperatures this afternoon will shift smoothly into a clear
pleasant evening with morning clouds along the coast and a clear

ridge over the area tonight will begin to shift into Eastern

A weak upper level trough with limited moisture will reach the
area Sunday with increasing onshore flow, more cloud cover

into the low to mid 70s and mid 60s for the coast.
