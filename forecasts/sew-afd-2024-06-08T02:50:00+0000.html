From Previous Discussion...Sunny skies and warmer temperatures this
afternoon will shift smoothly into a clear pleasant evening with
morning clouds along the coast and a clear mostly sunning morning
for the remaining area. The Upper level ridge over the area tonight
will begin to shift into Eastern Washington Saturday. Low level flow
will fully turn onshore in the afternoon with temperatures similar
to today over the interior with another round of 70s to lower 80s.
Temperatures along the coast will cool back down into the 60s.

A weak upper level trough with limited moisture will reach the area
Sunday with increasing onshore flow, more cloud cover Saturday night
into Sunday and a chance showers and a 15-20% chance of isolated
thunder in the Cascades Sunday. High temperatures on Sunday will
trend to the low to mid 70s over the interior and low to mid 60s
along the coast. Monday will trend drier with temperatures remaining
above normal for the interior, into the low to mid 70s and mid 60s
for the coast.
